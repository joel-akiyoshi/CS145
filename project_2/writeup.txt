Project 2: Digital Clock

Components:
- ATMEGA32 microcontroller
- LCD screen
- 4 x 4 keypad
- Resistors & wires
- Voltage Regulator
- 9V Battery
- 8 mHz crystal
- Programmer

Description:
The LCD and keypad function as a customizable clock that displays date in YYYY/MM/DD format, and the time in HH:MM:SS.
Both the date and time are programmable using the "D" and "A" keys, respectively. 

Notes:
The schematic is contained in the "images" folder of the project_2 directory.
