Project 3: Digital Clock

Components:
- ATMEGA32 microcontroller
- LCD screen
- 4 x 4 keypad
- Resistors & wires
- Voltage Regulator
- 9V Battery
- 8 mHz crystal
- Programmer
- Speaker

Description:
The main purpose of the project was to design a music box using a speaker, keypad, and LCD. Songs are selected by the keypad, displayed on
the LCD, and played through the speaker. Stop/start functionality is included by pressing A/B keys, and songs are selected using keys 1/2.
